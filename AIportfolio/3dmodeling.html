Photogrammetry, 3D Modeling and Rendering

MAYA | MESHMIXER | TRNIO

Steven Canoy (2021) Hologram Watch 960 x 540


In this piece I practiced Photogrammetry and 3D scanning with an app on my iPhone XR called "Trnio". I used my coworker as a model to scan. The scan was not as detailed as Agisoft Metashape or Reality Capture . and the model came out smooth and a little decimated. I decided to make him look polished like a metal statue. The background was a stretched image that gave a blur effect as if we took a photo focused on the subject giving him a realistic scene. wq

3D Modeling and Rendering

MAYA

Steven Canoy (2021) Food for Thought 1920 X 1080


For this piece I created a virtual reality in Maya. I wanted to explore textures and UV mapping images on polygonal models. Food for Thought is a surreal environment of giant foods that feed my guilty pleasures. As I get older, I find myself thinking more about my choice of food; therefore, I created giant foods falling into a morphed hole with "The Thinker" looking as if he is contemplating the bad decisions.

3D Modeling

MINECRAFT

Steven Canoy (2021) Baby Yoda 1920 x 1200 (294 blocks)


This was my first-time creating art in a 3D environment. Minecraft is similar to low resolution pixelated images. The art reminds me of Sega Genesis or Gameboy games and how their characters used to look. I created a head of Grogu aka "Baby Yoda" as my first piece because at the time, The Mandalorian season two just came out.
