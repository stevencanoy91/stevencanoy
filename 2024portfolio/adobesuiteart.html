Glitch Art

Photoshop | Audacity

Steven Canoy (2021) Champions Touch 1079 x 808

I created this piece by data bending the raw image file through Audacity. The original image is of Tom Brady being helped to his car after a day of drinking at the Buccaneer's Championship Parade. I opened the original image in Photoshop and saved the RAW file. Then, I uploaded the RAW file to Audacity which is an application for music and sound. Next, I added an echo effect to the RAW data and saved the new RAW file. Finally, I uploaded the new RAW file back to photoshop and saw how the image was sort of echoed and the colors became inverted.

Glitch Art

Photoshop | Lightroom Clasic

Steven Canoy (2021) RGB and Me 1200 x 800

Lightroom Classic and Photoshop is where I can glitch images directly by their RGB values and invert the color of the image. I can also choose how far I want to shift a specific value. I can make them display close together or far apart like in RGB and Me. It is a fully controlled glitch method aside from the Audacity data bending method. I find it less time consuming and easier to achieve the look I wanted.
