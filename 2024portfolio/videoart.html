PREMIERE PRO | AFTER EFFECTS

Steven Canoy (2021) Love & Dogs 1920 x1080 (00:02:06)

This video explores the possibilities of keyframing with the draw animation tool in After Effects. Learning how to keep lines in order was the biggest challenge because the videos were recorded without a tripod. I wanted to show my point of view and what I wake up to everyday. I'm very thankful for the things and life, I have created. Adding effects, colors, and music to the video made everything come together collectively as a representation of my positive attitude and spirit.

PREMIERE PRO

Steven Canoy (2021) Current Culture 1920 x 1080 (00:02:01)

The culture in our community has been at a great divide. I find peace when I get away from society. This projects intention was to learn how to piece together separate clips and images in Premiere Pro, to show the current state of our nation with a reminder of how beautiful this planet can be. At the time, we were and still are, dealing with racism, hate crimes, mass shootings, and a President who was showing little empathy for Charlottesville and the riots. A few things have changed, but some things still remain.

Steven Canoy (2021) The Rush 1920 x 1080 (00:02:18)

This was my first-time recording videos and uploading them to Premiere Pro for editing. I wanted to simulate the rush of emotions and feelings you get when playing Call of Duty. At the time, this was one of the most popular games being played. I show the viewer how your heart can start pounding in the middle of a battle with the urge to stay alive. Nobody likes losing but the worst feeling is losing connection.

PREMIERE PRO

Steven Canoy (2021) Kaleidoscope 1920 x 1080 (00:00:30)

I created a kaleidoscope effect with Premiere Pro by introducing mirrors to my scene. I made a blue bar rotate in a circle and drew and random pink line to complement the color. Making the mirrors act as walls is a great way to make an optical illusion out of a simple image. The music compliments the artwork because I set the rotation as the same BPM as the song.
